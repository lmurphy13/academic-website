I am studying under the supervision of Dr. Dennis Brylow in the Department of Computer Science. The area of my research is in compiler construction. For my MS thesis topic, I am endeavoring to improve a MiniJava compiler used in teaching the undergraduate and graduate Compiler Construction course at Marquette University.

This site serves as a place to record my progress, keep notes, and maintain a list of sources. If you find this site and wish to ask me about my research, please sent me an email at liam dot murphy at marquette dot edu.

I am also interested in embedded systems, systems programming, Linux development, security, privacy, and data ethics.
